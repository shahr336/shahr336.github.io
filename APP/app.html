What is My Manager?

My Manager is directly in sync with your university, keeping track of your classes, grades, academic events and most importantly, your valuable TIME.

Classes:

Find out when your classes are, where they are, who your professor is and the homework due for each class by using this super helpful tab.

Academic Calendar:

This calendar is created to show you the most important university holidays, when your homework is due and also your to-do list for the day.

Time Tracker:

The highlight of this application, the time tracker helps you manage your time effortlessly. Simply set a new bedtime to start the countdown, and the Time Manager will automatically prepare a pie chart showing you how to distribute your day evenly.

To-do List:

This simple and straightforward to-do list is also in sync with the Academic Calendar, making sure you never miss a task. To add a new to-do simply click on the edit button.
